About the Show

Groovie Dance Show is a music and dance entertainment program that is being exclusively broadcasted as one of the premium channels in Vuuzle.TV OTT platform—an original show production of the triple-play company Vuuzle Media Corp. It features hit dances of all time that made everyone groovie and dancing! Groovie Dance Show is inspired by the old show called “Soul Train”. The show aspires to bring back the old excitement of everyone in every household around the world by watching artists performing hit and timeless dance moves with a touch of the millennial generation.
